Who We Are

LYRĪON is a celestial couture house — where light meets form. We craft quiet, durable pieces that carry the rhythm of the sky into everyday rituals. Born from a love of astrology, design, and conscious living, each collection honors the zodiac's twelve archetypes through premium apparel, homeware, and altar pieces.

We are makers, dreamers, and seekers — guided by the stars, grounded in craft.

Materials & Making

  • Premium organic cotton and blended fabrics sourced for longevity and comfort.
  • Archival-grade threads and museum-quality inks ensure colors remain vibrant through years of wear.
  • Framed prints, candles, and homeware crafted with natural materials and sustainable practices.
  • On-demand production across trusted partners minimizes waste and ensures each piece is made with intention.

Care & Intention

Every LYRĪON piece is designed to be lived in, loved, and cared for:

  • Wear often. Wash cool. Line dry when possible.
  • Burn candles with care — trim wicks to ¼ inch and never leave unattended.
  • Handle prints by their edges; display away from direct sunlight to preserve archival quality.
  • Treat each object as you would a ritual tool — with attention, respect, and gentle use.
