Frequently Asked

How are readings made?

Each reading is handwritten with intention, guided by astrological timing, intuitive pulls, and the resonance of your question. We blend traditional astrology with quiet observation and symbolic interpretation.

When to expect a reply?

We typically respond within 24–72 hours. During celestial events (new moons, eclipses, retrogrades), replies may take a bit longer as we honor the timing of the cosmos.

Privacy & data

Your questions and personal details remain confidential. We do not share, sell, or store your information beyond what's needed to reply. All correspondence is treated with sacred privacy.

Is this religious?

No. LYRĪON's practices are intentionally non-religious—they are about resonance, attention, and presence. We honor all beliefs and do not promote any doctrine.

Refunds & adjustments
