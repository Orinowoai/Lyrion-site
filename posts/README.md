## Content Strategy

### Publishing Rhythm
- **Frequency:** Bi-weekly (aiming for 2 essays per month)
- **Length:** 1,200-2,000 words (7-12 minute read)
- **Tone:** Literary, elegant, commanding but not preachy
- **SEO:** Naturally optimized through quality content, not keyword stuffing

### Distribution Channels
1. **Website Blog:** Primary home, full text
2. **Email Newsletter:** Excerpts with "read more" links
3. **Instagram:** Quote graphics from essays

## Editorial Guidelines

### Voice & Tone
- **Authoritative but not academic** — We know our subject deeply but explain clearly
- **Poetic but not purple** — Beautiful language serving meaning, not obscuring it
- **Inclusive but not pandering** — Welcoming without dumbing down
- **Honest about limits** — We're not scientists or priestesses; we're guides

### Structure Template
1. **Opening hook:** Immediate engagement (question, scene, provocation)
2. **Thesis statement:** What this essay explores
3. **Historical/cultural context:** Grounding in larger tradition
4. **LYRĪON perspective:** How we interpret/apply this
5. **Practical application:** What the reader can do
6. **Invitation to practice:** Ending with action or reflection
7. **Signature line:** Always end with brand essence tagline

### SEO Best Practices (Non-Intrusive)
- Natural keyword inclusion in headers and first paragraph
- Alt text for any images (descriptive, poetic)
- Internal links to product pages (contextual, never forced)
- Meta descriptions that compel clicks without clickbait
- Long-tail keywords naturally embedded ("how to dress for your zodiac sign" not "zodiac fashion")

## Cross-Promotion Opportunities
