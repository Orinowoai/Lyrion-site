Frequently Asked

What exactly does my donation support?

Offerings sustain the daily and weekly practices of the circle: keeping names, composing ritual letters, lighting intention candles, and preserving time for attentive care and small acts of ritual that carry energy forward.

Will I receive a receipt?

Yes. Payment platforms (Stripe/Ko‑fi) will provide an email receipt automatically. For bespoke receipts or fellowship records, contact hello@lyrion.co.uk and we will assist.

Is this religious?

No. LYRĪON’s practices are intentionally non-religious — they are about resonance, attention, and presence. We honor all beliefs and do not promote any doctrine.
